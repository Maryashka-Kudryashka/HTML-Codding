What's been happening? We're all pruned and like clockwork buds burst on 22 September, being the spring equinox. With such a wet winter here we are ready for a busy spring, under-vine weeding and starting to shoot thin. We are now fully certified organic with BioGro (as of March 2017). Our BioGro number is 5202. It's great to get the tick. Onwards!

Damsteep Pinot Noir 2015

Deep ruby red hue. Crushed raspberry, red currant jelly, sandstone and white rock. Silken, rich and expansive fruit flow across the palate with savory underlying tannin giving tension and power. Flavors of dark clay with a strong mineral line that is saline and persistent give an impression of white rock and elevation. Drink now until 2026.

$45.00

Home Pinot Noir 2015

Red and black forest fruit, with black current and raspberry, wild rose and subtle fresh spice. Berry fruits are rich and broad, sensual and luscious with underlying savoury earth and gentle but persistent minerality. 

$45.00

Netherwood Pinot Noir 2014

Earth, cherries, tamarillo, spice, licorice, ash and red roses on the nose. The wine is dense with  rich forest fruits, red cherries, roses and damson plum. Supple and billowing with a vibrant sandstone line. Salivating. Drink until 2026.

$65.00
